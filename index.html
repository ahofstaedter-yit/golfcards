Golf Course Collection

One of the most celebrated courses on the PGA TOUR, Harbour Town is both the crowning achievement of famed designer Pete Dye and design consultant Jack Nicklaus.

It places a premium on finesse, imagination and shot making, rather than strength.

Pebble Beach is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful courses in the world.

Since opening in 1919, this legendary links-style course has hosted numerous U.S. Opens.

Home of the Masters Tournament, Augusta National is one of the most exclusive and prestigious golf clubs in the world.

The course features iconic holes like Amen Corner and beautiful azalea-lined fairways.

Known as the 'Home of Golf', the Old Course at St Andrews has been played since the 15th century.

Its unique double greens, deep bunkers, and unpredictable Scottish weather make it a true test of golf.

Designed by Donald Ross, Pinehurst No. 2 is known for its crowned greens and strategic bunkering.

It has hosted more single golf championships than any other course in America, including multiple U.S. Opens.
